Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

Q

What is Z.ONE?

A

Z.ONE is a Trading community focused on learning, sharing, market updates, and growing together from zero to one.

Q

Is Z.ONE suitable for complete beginners?

A

Yes. Z.ONE is built with the “Zero to One” idea in mind, so even if you have no background, you can start learning step by step.

Q

Is the community on Discord?

A

Yes. The community is hosted on Discord, but users contact us first before being added manually.

Q

What kind of content do you share?

A

We provide systematic trading courses (MACD, SMC, Price Action), live market analysis, mid-to-long-term trend planning, capital management education, Bot-powered news alerts, and community discussion.

Q

How are the courses structured?

A

We offer a Zero-to-One foundation class for beginners, as well as advanced strategy combinations for experienced traders. The goal is to develop independent traders who can analyze the market on their own.
